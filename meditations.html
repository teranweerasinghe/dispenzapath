Beginner Meditations

Morning Blessing of the Energy Centers

Beginner · Phase 1
Duration: ~45 min Frequency: Daily Where to find: drjoedispenza.com

This is your first and most important meditation. It systematically moves awareness through each of the 8 energy centers while cultivating elevated emotions associated with each one. It builds the foundational skill of sustained attention and begins the process of clearing suppressed energy from the centers.

The most common mistake here is "going through the motions" — repeating the words and visualizations without actually generating the corresponding emotion. Dispenza is explicit: feeling is the signal, not the visualization. If you're not feeling anything, slow down and stay at one center until something shifts.

Beginner tip: The first 14 days, keep your eyes closed and sit in a chair (not lying down — you will fall asleep). Morning means before your first screen. This single commitment, held consistently, produces more results than any other combination of content.

Body-Part Tuning In

Beginner · Phase 2
Duration: ~30 minFrequency: 3–4x per weekAdd after: 14 days of Morning Blessing

This meditation develops interoception — your ability to sense inside your body. You progressively move awareness to different body parts, which trains the anterior insular cortex and builds the internal sensing capacity required for the more advanced energy work.

Why this comes before Space Awareness: You need to know what it feels like to be inside your body before you can practice expanding awareness beyond it. Skipping this creates a conceptual understanding without the felt sense.

Intermediate Meditations

Space Awareness Meditation

Intermediate · Phase 2
Duration: ~45–60 minFrequency: 3–4x per weekPrerequisite: 4+ weeks of daily morning practice

This is where the real identity shift begins. Space awareness trains you to place your attention in the space around your body — and then to expand that space infinitely in all directions. The result is a dissolution of the body's perceived boundaries, which Dispenza connects to entering the quantum field.

This meditation is the gateway to theta states. Most people who report profound experiences credit this meditation as the turning point. It is also the most commonly rushed — people want the experience before they've built the capacity.

Common mistake: Attempting this meditation before having a stable daily practice. Without the foundation, your analytical mind will simply keep commenting on the experience rather than releasing into it.

Walking Meditations

Intermediate · Phase 3
Duration: ~20–40 minFrequency: 3x per weekPrerequisite: Phase 2 complete

Walking meditations bridge the sitting practice into daily life. They train you to maintain elevated emotional states while the body is in motion and the senses are receiving external information — a much harder neurological task than sitting in silence. This is the Phase 3 skill that makes the advanced work possible.

Practical setup: Download the audio, use wireless headphones, find a quiet outdoor space. Don't try to do this in an urban environment during your first 4 weeks. Natural surroundings significantly reduce the load on the suppression system.

Advanced Meditations

Pineal Gland Meditation

Advanced · Phase 4
Duration: ~60–90 minFrequency: As directed in coursePrerequisite: Phases 1–3 complete

This is Dispenza's most advanced meditation, working with the pineal gland as what he describes as a "third eye" transducer of higher-dimensional information. It involves prolonged theta states, internal breath work (a form of Kundalini-adjacent technique), and precise energy directing through the brain.

The experiences reported from this meditation — light phenomena, out-of-body sensations, profound peace — are real and documented in his EEG studies. They are also meaningless without the conceptual and experiential foundation of the earlier phases.

Do not start here: This is the meditation most new students want to try first because it sounds the most dramatic. This is precisely why most of them report nothing happening. The pineal gland work requires a nervous system that has been trained to sustain altered states for extended periods. Build that first.

Coherence Healing

Advanced · Phase 4
Duration: ~60 minFrequency: Monthly community sessionsAvailable: Online group sessions

Coherence Healing is a group practice where experienced practitioners enter elevated states collectively and direct healing intention toward a recipient. The science here references quantum entanglement and field effects. Whether or not you accept the mechanism, the documented results from his community are striking.

How to access: Coherence healing events are hosted through Dispenza's online community and at live events. You can participate as recipient or practitioner (practitioner requires advanced training).
