Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 1, 2024

Dispenza Path ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

Information We Collect

Newsletter subscriptions: If you subscribe to our email newsletter, we collect your email address. We use this solely to send the weekly newsletter. We do not sell, share, or trade email addresses with third parties.

Analytics: We may collect anonymized usage data (pages visited, time on site, device type) through privacy-respecting analytics tools. This data contains no personally identifiable information.

Cookies: We use minimal session cookies necessary for the site to function. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used only to deliver the Dispenza Path newsletter. We will never use it for advertising, sell it to data brokers, or share it with Joe Dispenza's organization or any commercial entity.

Your Rights

You may unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email. You may request deletion of your email address at any time by contacting us. If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, and portability of your data.

Third-Party Links

This site links to external sites including drjoedispenza.com, gaia.com, and Amazon. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Affiliate Disclosure

Some links on this site may be affiliate links. This means if you purchase a product through our link, we may receive a small commission at no additional cost to you. This does not influence our editorial recommendations — we only recommend products we genuinely believe are the right ones for the appropriate stage of the learning path.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.
